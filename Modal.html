Examples

A modal is used to add dialogs into your web page, many different styles of it.


Live Demo

A live demo modal is a button that by clicking it will lead to an alert.


Static Backdrop

When it is static it means that when you click the button and it shows the alert you won be able to click out of it unless you click the "X" on the top right of the alert.


Toggle Between Modals

Toggle between multiple modals means that you can go from the first alert to then the second by just a click of a button.
